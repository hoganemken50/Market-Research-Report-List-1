<p><p>Social advertising tools can be classified into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based social advertising tools are hosted on remote servers, allowing users access via the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and easier collaboration, making them suitable for businesses of all sizes. Conversely, on-premise tools are installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control, data security, and customization. These tools may require more IT resources but are preferred by organizations with strict compliance and data management policies.</p></p>
