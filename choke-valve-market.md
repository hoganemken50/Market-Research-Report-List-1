<p><strong>The Choke Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight-through Type</li><li>Angle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The choke valve market includes two primary types: straight-through and angle type. Straight-through choke valves facilitate a direct flow path, minimizing pressure drop and turbulence, making them ideal for applications requiring high flow rates. In contrast, angle choke valves direct the flow at a 90-degree angle, which can be beneficial for space-constrained installations. Both types serve critical roles in throttling and controlling fluid flow in various industries, including oil and gas, water management, and chemical processing.</p></p>
