<p><p>Sustainable Electronic Aviation Fuel (ESAF) encompasses two main market types: Sustainable Aviation Biofuels and Sustainable Aviation Synthetic Fuel. Sustainable Aviation Biofuels are derived from renewable biomass sources, such as plant oils and agricultural waste, reducing greenhouse gas emissions compared to traditional jet fuels. In contrast, Sustainable Aviation Synthetic Fuel is produced using advanced technologies, like power-to-liquid processes, converting renewable energy into liquid hydrocarbons. Both types aim to decarbonize aviation and promote eco-friendly air travel, supporting sustainability goals in the industry.</p></p>
