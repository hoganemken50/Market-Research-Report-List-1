<p><strong>The Mountain Bike Shock Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Shock</li><li>Coil Shock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike shock market primarily features two types: air shocks and coil shocks. Air shocks utilize air pressure for adjustable damping, offering lightweight performance and tunability, making them suitable for cross-country and trail riding. In contrast, coil shocks rely on metal springs for consistent and reliable damping, delivering superior performance on rough terrain, favored by downhill and enduro riders. Each type caters to specific riding styles, with preferences influenced by factors like weight, terrain, and the desired riding experience.</p></p>
