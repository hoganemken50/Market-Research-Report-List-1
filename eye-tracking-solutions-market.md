<p><strong>The Eye Tracking Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eye Attached Tracking</li><li>Optical Tracking</li><li>Electrooculography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eye tracking solutions market comprises three main types: Eye Attached Tracking, Optical Tracking, and Electrooculography. Eye Attached Tracking involves wearable devices that monitor eye movements closely, often used in user experience research. Optical Tracking utilizes external cameras to analyze eye movements without requiring direct contact, suitable for various applications like gaming and virtual reality. Electrooculography measures electrical potential changes in the eye muscles, providing high precision for clinical and research purposes, particularly in neurological studies.</p></p>
