<p><p>The auto exhaust system market primarily features two types: single exhaust systems and dual exhaust systems. A single exhaust system utilizes one exhaust pipe to expel exhaust gases, offering simpler design and cost-effectiveness, commonly found in smaller vehicles. In contrast, dual exhaust systems employ two separate pipes, enhancing engine performance and sound by allowing better exhaust flow, typically seen in performance and luxury vehicles. Both systems significantly impact vehicle efficiency, emissions, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
