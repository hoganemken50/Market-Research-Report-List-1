<p><p>The expanding plug valves market includes two primary types: handwheel-operated and gear-operated valves. Handwheel-operated valves utilize a manual handwheel for operation, providing direct and straightforward control for operators. In contrast, gear-operated valves use a gear mechanism to facilitate easier operation, often suitable for larger valves or applications requiring more torque. Both types are designed for applications needing reliable shut-off and flow control, catering to various industries such as oil and gas, water management, and chemical processing.</p></p>
