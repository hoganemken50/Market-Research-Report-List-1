<p><p>The non-commercial acrylic paints market encompasses various types designed for artists and hobbyists. Fluid acrylics are highly pigmented and have a thin consistency, ideal for pouring techniques. Heavy-body acrylics offer a thicker texture, allowing for bold, impasto effects and brushwork. Slow-drying agents are additives that extend the working time of acrylic paints, enabling smoother blending and manipulation. Together, these products enhance creative possibilities for artists by providing a range of textures, consistencies, and drying times to suit different techniques.</p></p>
