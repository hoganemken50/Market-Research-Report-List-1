<p><strong>The Smart Automotive Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access</li><li>Remote Keyless Access</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Automotive Key market is primarily categorized into two types: Passive Keyless Access (PKA) and Remote Keyless Access (RKA). PKA systems allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically using a key, enhancing convenience and security through proximity sensors. RKA, on the other hand, requires the user to press a button on a remote key fob to unlock and start the vehicle, providing ease of use but at a slightly lower level of automation compared to PKA systems.</p></p>
