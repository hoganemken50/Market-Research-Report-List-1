<p><p>The Genechip Scanner market features two primary types: Laser Co-Aggregation Chip Scanners and CCD Chip Scanners. Laser Co-Aggregation Chip Scanners utilize laser technology to finely analyze gene expression through high-resolution imaging, ensuring precise data collection. On the other hand, CCD Chip Scanners employ charge-coupled device technology for capturing images and measuring fluorescence, offering enhanced sensitivity and faster image acquisition. Both types serve crucial roles in genomics research, facilitating advancements in personalized medicine and molecular biology.</p></p>
