SPLDs are used for straightforward logic functions; CPLDs provide more complex designs with multiple logic blocks and a shorter propagation delay. FPGAs offer extensive configurability, enabling intricate designs and parallel processing. The FPD market caters to various industries, driven by the demand for customizable and efficient hardware solutions in electronic systems.</p></p>
