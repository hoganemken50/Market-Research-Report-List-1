<p><ul><li>Natural Rubber</li><li>Synthetic Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber flooring tiles market is primarily categorized into two types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber tiles are derived from the sap of rubber trees, offering excellent elasticity and durability, making them ideal for home gyms and commercial spaces. In contrast, synthetic rubber tiles are manufactured from petroleum-based products, providing enhanced resistance to chemicals, moisture, and wear. Both types cater to diverse applications, including fitness centers, playgrounds, and industrial settings, highlighting their unique properties and durability.</p></p>
