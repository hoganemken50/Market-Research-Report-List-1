<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detector</li><li>Active Leak Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water leak detector market comprises two main types: passive and active leak detectors. Passive leak detectors are non-intrusive devices that rely on sensors to detect the presence of water, alerting users to potential leaks without actively monitoring. In contrast, active leak detectors are equipped with automatic shut-off mechanisms that not only identify leaks but also take immediate action, such as stopping water flow to prevent damage. Both types are essential for protecting property and minimizing water-related risks.</p></p>
