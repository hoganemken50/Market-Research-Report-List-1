<p><p>The surgical guidewires market is categorized into three main types: nitinol guidewires, stainless steel guidewires, and hybrid guidewires. Nitinol guidewires are made from a nickel-titanium alloy, providing flexibility and shape memory, making them ideal for complex procedures. Stainless steel guidewires offer strength and rigidity, suitable for straightforward interventions. Hybrid guidewires combine properties of both materials, aiming to enhance performance for specific applications. Each type serves distinct roles in various surgical specialties, catering to diverse procedural needs.</p></p>
