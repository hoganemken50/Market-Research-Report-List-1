<p><p>Sophorolipids are biosurfactants derived from the fermentation of sugars by certain yeast strains, mainly Candida bombicola. They are characterized by their unique molecular structure, which comprises a sugar moiety and a fatty acid component. Furthermore, the food and beverage industry is witnessing a shift towards natural emulsifiers and stabilizers, enhancing the demand for sophorolipids.
