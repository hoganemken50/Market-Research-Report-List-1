<p><p>The electric actuated butterfly valve market is segmented into rubber sealed and metal sealed types. In contrast, metal sealed valves feature metallic sealing surfaces that offer enhanced durability and are suitable for high-pressure environments. Both types are essential in various industries, facilitating efficient flow control in pipelines and systems requiring precise regulation.</p></p>
