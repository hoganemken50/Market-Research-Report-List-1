Mono-block manifolds are single-piece designs that provide a compact and robust solution for fluid control, ideal for high-pressure applications. Conversely, modular manifold blocks consist of multiple interchangeable components, offering flexibility and scalability for diverse hydraulic systems. This adaptability allows for easy customization to meet specific system requirements, making modular configurations popular in applications requiring frequent modifications or upgrades.</p></p>
