<p><strong>The Wafer Inspection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Wafer Inspection</li><li>E-Beam Wafer Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer inspection device market includes two primary types: optical wafer inspection and e-beam wafer inspection. Optical wafer inspection utilizes light to detect defects on semiconductor wafers, offering fast, high-throughput analysis with the ability to inspect large areas efficiently. In contrast, e-beam wafer inspection employs electron beams for higher resolution imaging, enabling the identification of smaller defects with greater precision but typically at slower speeds and higher costs. Both methods are essential for quality assurance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
