<p><p>The elevator safety system market encompasses three primary types: control systems, maintenance systems, and communication systems. Control systems manage elevator operations, ensuring safe and efficient movement. Maintenance systems monitor the elevator's health, scheduling regular inspections and repairs to prevent malfunctions. Communication systems facilitate real-time updates and emergency alerts between elevators and monitoring personnel, enhancing passenger safety. Together, these components work seamlessly to minimize risks, improve reliability, and ensure a safe user experience in vertical transportation.</p></p>
