<p><strong>The Paint Booths Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Type Paint Booths</li><li>Closed Type Paint Booths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint booths market is primarily divided into open-type and closed-type paint booths. Open-type paint booths feature minimal enclosure, allowing for better ventilation and airflow, making them suitable for outdoor or well-ventilated spaces. They are typically more cost-effective but provide less protection against contaminants. Closed-type paint booths offer a controlled environment, featuring sealed walls, filtration systems, and temperature control, ensuring high-quality finishes and reduced pollution. These booths are ideal for industries requiring precision and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
