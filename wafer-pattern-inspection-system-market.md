<p><strong>The Wafer Pattern Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Inspection System</li><li>Bright-Field Inspection System</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer Pattern Inspection Systems are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, utilizing various techniques to ensure quality. Electron Beam Inspection Systems use focused electron beams to achieve high resolution, detecting defects at nanometer levels. Bright-Field Inspection Systems illuminate the wafer directly, observing surface patterns and defects under visible light, suitable for larger defects. Conversely, Dark-Field Inspection Systems utilize scattered light to highlight defects against a dark background, effectively revealing irregularities. Together, these systems enhance yield and reliability in chip production.</p></p>
