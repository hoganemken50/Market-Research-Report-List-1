<p><p>The Clinical Trial Imaging Service market consists of two main types: In-house Imaging Service and Central Imaging Service. In-house Imaging Service involves facilities where clinical trial imaging is conducted within the sponsor’s or site’s premises, ensuring direct control over the imaging process and immediate access to data. Conversely, Central Imaging Service operates through dedicated imaging centers that specialize in processing images from multiple trial sites, providing standardized methodologies and enhancing data consistency across diverse geographic locations.</p></p>
