<p><p>The automotive air filter market comprises two main types: intake air filters and cabin air filters. Intake air filters clean the air entering the engine, ensuring optimal combustion and enhancing performance by preventing contaminants from damaging engine components. Cabin air filters, on the other hand, purify the air inside the vehicle, trapping dust, pollen, and pollutants to enhance passenger comfort and health. Both filters are essential for vehicle maintenance, improving efficiency, and ensuring a pleasant driving experience.</p></p>
