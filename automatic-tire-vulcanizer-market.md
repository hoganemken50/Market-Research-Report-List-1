<p><strong>The Automatic Tire Vulcanizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical vulcanizer</li><li>Hydraulic vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Tire Vulcanizer market comprises two main types: mechanical and hydraulic vulcanizers. Mechanical vulcanizers utilize a system of levers and gears to create pressure and heat, facilitating the curing process. They are generally simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, hydraulic vulcanizers employ hydraulic systems to exert pressure, allowing for more precise control and uniform heating. This results in better-quality tire curing, making hydraulic models preferable for high-volume or specialized operations in the tire manufacturing industry.</p></p>
