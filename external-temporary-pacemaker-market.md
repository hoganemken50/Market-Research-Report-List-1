<p><p>The external temporary pacemaker market is segmented into two main types: dual chamber and single chamber external temporary pacemakers. A dual chamber external temporary pacemaker stimulates both the atria and ventricles, providing coordinated control of heart rhythms, making it ideal for more complex cardiac conditions. In contrast, a single chamber external temporary pacemaker targets either the atria or ventricles, offering a simpler solution for patients needing basic pacing support. Both devices are crucial for temporary cardiac support in critical care settings.</p></p>
