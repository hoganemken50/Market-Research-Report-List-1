<p><p>Grain engineering services encompass three primary market types: handling, storage, and processing. Storage focuses on maintaining grain quality during holding periods, utilizing specialized facilities to prevent spoilage and pests. Processing encompasses the transformation of raw grain into value-added products, involving milling, refining, and packaging.
