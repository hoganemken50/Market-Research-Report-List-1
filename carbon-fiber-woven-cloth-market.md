<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Weaving</li><li>Two-way Weaving</li><li>Multiaxial Weaving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber woven cloth market is categorized into three main types based on weaving techniques. Unidirectional weaving involves fibers aligned in a single direction, providing high strength and stiffness along that axis. Two-way weaving features fibers arranged in both horizontal and vertical orientations, enhancing overall fabric stability and force distribution. Multiaxial weaving incorporates layers of fibers oriented in multiple directions, offering improved structural performance and versatility for complex applications. Each type caters to specific industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
