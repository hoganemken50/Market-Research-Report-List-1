<p><p>The Distributed I/O Module market is categorized into digital and analog segments. Digital I/O modules handle discrete signals, typically used for binary operations, such as on/off switches and sensors, providing quick response times. Conversely, analog I/O modules process continuous signals, capturing variations in voltage or current, and are essential for applications requiring precise measurements, like temperature or pressure monitoring. Both types play a critical role in automation systems, ensuring efficient data acquisition and control across various industrial processes.</p></p>
