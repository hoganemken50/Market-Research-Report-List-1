<p><strong>The Radiation-Hardened Electronics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD)</li><li>Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation-hardened electronics market is primarily categorized into two types: Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD) and Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP). RHBD involves designing electronic components to effectively withstand radiation effects through architectural modifications and redundancy. In contrast, RHBP focuses on manufacturing techniques that enhance the inherent resistance of devices to radiation, such as specialized materials and fabrication processes. Both approaches aim to ensure reliability and functionality of electronics in high-radiation environments, such as space and nuclear applications.</p></p>
