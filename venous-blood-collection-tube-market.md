<p><p>The venous blood collection tube market includes three primary applications: anticoagulants, coagulants, and no additives. Anticoagulant tubes are used to prevent blood clotting, making them ideal for tests requiring plasma, such as genetic testing or metabolic panels. Coagulant tubes facilitate the clotting of blood, useful for serum analysis in diagnostic testing. Tubes with no additives are employed when the sample's integrity needs to be preserved for certain evaluations. Each type serves crucial roles in laboratory diagnostics and blood analysis.</p></p>
