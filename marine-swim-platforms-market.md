<p><p>Marine swim platforms are categorized into three main types: fixed, floating, and others. Fixed platforms are permanently anchored structures that provide stable access to water, often seen in resorts or private properties. Floating platforms, buoyant by design, can adapt to changing water levels, making them suitable for various water bodies, including lakes and coastal areas. The "others" category includes unique designs or specialized applications, such as inflatable platforms or temporary installations, catering to specific needs or events in aquatic environments.</p></p>
