Built steel I-beams are assembled from plates and are tailored for specific load requirements, allowing for customization in heavy-duty scenarios. External constant steel I-beams maintain a consistent cross-section throughout their length, providing uniform strength and efficiency in applications where dimensional stability is crucial, making them ideal for long spans and heavy loads.</p></p>
