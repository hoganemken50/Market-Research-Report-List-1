<p><ul><li>Combo Amplifiers</li><li>Head Amplifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guitar amplifier market primarily consists of two types: combo amplifiers and head amplifiers. Combo amplifiers integrate the amplifier and speaker into a single unit, making them portable and user-friendly, ideal for practice and small gigs. In contrast, head amplifiers consist solely of the amplifier module, requiring separate speaker cabinets for sound output, offering enhanced versatility and sound quality for professional settings. Both types cater to different player needs, from casual musicians to seasoned performers.</p></p>
