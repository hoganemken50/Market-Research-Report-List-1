<p><p>Tumor blood testing, also known as liquid biopsy, is a diagnostic method that involves analyzing circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA), circulating tumor cells (CTCs), and other tumor-related biomarkers in a patient's blood. The method is particularly advantageous as it minimizes the need for invasive tissue biopsies, facilitating more accessible and timely diagnosis.</p><p>The Tumor Blood Testing Market is experiencing significant growth driven by advancements in genomic technologies, increasing prevalence of cancer, and the rising demand for personalized medicine.
